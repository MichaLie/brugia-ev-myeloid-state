# Supplemental Figure Legends

## Figure S1. Gene-level TLR projection detail across stimuli and myeloid cell types.

**(A)** Heatmap of gene-level TLR-induced log2 fold changes for frozen-signature genes in monocytes under TLR7/8 and TLR4 stimulation. **(B)** Relationship between mean EV log2 fold change and monocyte TLR7/8 log2 fold change, colored by deterministic module. Module B preferentially occupies the discordant quadrant. **(C)** Module-level concordance fractions across stimuli and myeloid cell types.

## Figure S2. Expanded regulatory-structure and module-robustness analyses.

**(A)** Expanded transcription-factor enrichment view showing the strongest frozen arm-specific TF signals after false-discovery filtering. **(B)** Held-out gene-level concordance across deterministic modules in resting and LPS-background transfer contexts. Module B remains the most independently robust component.

## Figure S3. Gene-level orthogonal validation detail for `GSE187403` and `GSE143170`.

Scatter plots compare mean EV log2 fold change for each frozen-signature gene against orthogonal perturbation log2 fold change in `GSE187403` (`LPS vs media`; `AZD2014+LPS vs LPS`) and `GSE143170` (`temsirolimus vs pH 7.3`; `pH 6.5 vs pH 7.3`). Blue points denote down-arm genes and vermillion points denote up-arm genes. These panels visualize the preferential recovery of the down arm by acute innate stimulation and the context-dependent recovery of the MYC-associated arm by temsirolimus.

## Figure S4. Full external transport and standardized score-delta detail.

**(A)** Total concordance fractions for every external contrast evaluated in the direct-exposure, infection, same-parasite, and cross-helminth datasets. **(B)** Heatmap of within-cohort standardized score deltas for the full Core EV score and the deterministic modules. Positive values indicate EV-consistent transport after sign standardization, and negative values indicate divergence from the EV direction.

## Figure S5. Tolerance-neighborhood and comparator-context detail.

**(A)** Overlap of curated tolerance markers with the frozen signature; only `IRAK1` is retained in the reconstructed state. **(B)** Mean external-dataset consistency of the curated tolerance-marker set, showing that tolerance coherence does not track the EV signature in aggregate. **(C)** Contextual comparator view of mean Core EV scores in PDAC myeloid cohorts and across canonical macrophage-polarization states, emphasizing neighborhood-level resemblance without implying mechanistic equivalence.
